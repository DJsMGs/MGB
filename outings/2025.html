MGB Outings - 2025

MG Dealer, Bognor

The West Sussex MG Owners Club had been invited to take some 'old' MGs along to an open day at the MG dealer in Bognor, so it seemed like a good opportunity to take the B for a run. I could also ask other B owners some questions.

The only problem was that it was raining. In fact it rained all morning and I even had to drive through a flood (the road was closed later). However, the car ran well and I didn't get wet. Some people went for a test drive in current-day 'Chinese' MGs.

SADCASE Gathering at Slindon, December

The weather forecast had said it would be fine day, and it was; sunshine from dawn to dusk. Since SADCASE were advertising a meet at the Spur in Slindon, it seemed like a good idea, not only to give the MGB an outing, but also to take Angela for her first run in the B.
