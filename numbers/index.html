Identification Numbers for DKK 416K

There are a group of numbers that specify the particular variant of MGB. This page states those numbers for DKK 416K and attempts to explain what they mean.

Manufacture/Registration Dates

Date built: 8th-9th March 1972

Date first registered: 18th April 1972

Car Number

GHN5276357G

According to the JustMGB.com web site:

G = MG
H = Engine Size between 1400cc & 2000cc
N = 2 seated Roadster
5276357 = Car number
G = Built at MG Factory Abingdon

Commission Number

G23N 050055 Z

According to Tony Barnhill “Commission numbers are irrelevant today...they were internal numbers used while a car was being built before the VIN was attached....MG just didn't remove them.”

From August 1972: G23N/nnnnn (According to Moss Motors)
Z - Pressed Steel, Cowley (Apparently according to 'ORIGINAL MGB WITH MGC & MGB GT V8' by Anders Clausager)

Body Number

As of December 2025 I have not found a Body Number on the car.

Engine Number

18V581-H2264

According to the JustMGB.com web site:

18V = 1800cc, in-line
581 = Twin HS4 carbs
H = High compressio
2264 = Serial number
